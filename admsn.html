Eligibility

  1. H.S (10+2) Examination of the Council of Higher Secondary Education, West Bengal or equivalent with Physics and Mathematics along with any one of Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology / Computer Science / Computer Application/ Technical Vocational Subject as compulsory subjects with individual pass marks (in both theory and practical wherever applicable) in all the three subjects as stated above in regular class mode.
  2. At least 30% marks in English and minimum 45% marks in the above three subjects taken together
  3. Qualified Joint Entrance Examination conducted by West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board, JEE (Main) conducted by NTA and recommended by the WBJEE Board.
  4. The Board of the said Qualifying Examination must be recognized by the Central Government or State Government concerned.

B.Tech (Lateral Entry)- 3 years

  1. Lateral Entry to second year B.Tech shall be permissible up to a maximum of 10% of the “approved intake” which shall be over and above, supernumerary to the “approved intake” plus unfilled vacancies of the first year.

Eligibility

  1. Qualified JELET conducted by West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board
  2. Diploma from an AICTE approved Institution with at least 45% or equivalent CGPA considering all semesters / parts in Diploma examination without any back paper(s) in appropriate branch of Engineering / Technology.

OR

  1. B.Sc. from any University recognized by UGC with at least 45% and Mathematics in 12th standard examination
